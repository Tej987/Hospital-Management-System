Overview

Dermatology is the branch of medicine that focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of skin disorders. It encompasses a wide range of conditions affecting the skin, hair, and nails, from acne and eczema to skin cancer and hair loss.

Our Dermatology department is equipped with the latest technologies and led by a team of expert dermatologists who are dedicated to providing the highest level of care. Whether you're dealing with a common skin condition or a more complex issue, our specialists are here to help.
